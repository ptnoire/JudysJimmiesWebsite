What Will You Be Having?

We proudly server Penn State Creamery's Ice Cream to beautiful Beaver County!

To get a more detailed description of any of our flavors available, just give a click on any of the ice cream below!

Alumni Swirl

"Our blue and white confection pays homage to the thousands of alumni who make it a point to visit the Creamery whenever they're on campus. We blend vanilla ice cream with Swiss mocha chips and blueberry swirl to create a dessert that's uniquely Penn State."

Butter Pecan

"It's no wonder this is one of America's longtime favorite flavors. Delicious, meaty pecan halves nestled into butter almond flavored ice cream? Deeply satisfying."

Happy Happy Joy Joy

"We call this ice cream "Happy Happy Joy Joy" because that's what folks tend to chant whenever they see it on our menu. Getting excited about sweet, tropical coconut ice cream studded with butter roasted almonds and chocolate chips? We get it."

Chocolate Marshmallow

"Curl up with a good book and a mug of our chocolate ice cream with marshmallow swirl. We promise you won't even miss the cocoa."

Monkey Business

"Our banana ice cream with peanut butter swirl and chocolate chips is a little sweet, a little silly, and seriously delicious!"

Death by Chocolate

"Flakes of pure chocolate, fudge pieces, and a chocolate swirl are blended with our rich, dense chocolate ice cream for a taste that’s to die for. So take a bite, close your eyes, and let us take you to a dark, dark place."

Peanut Butter Swirl

"Peanut butter swirl provides the perfect salty sweetness, while vanilla ice cream refreshes you with every bite."

Scholar's Chip

"Sweet and flavorful vanilla ice cream gets a boost from extra vanilla bean and chocolate chips."

Raspberry Fudge Torte

"Delicate, delicious and indulgent, our vanilla ice cream with fudge pieces and red raspberry swirl might make you feel just a teensy bit reckless. Go ahead, get another bowl."

Peachy Paterno

"In 1987, the Penn State Food Science Club held an ice cream naming contest to honor Joe Paterno's academic contributions to the university. Our peach flavored ice cream with delectable peach slices is a tribute to Joe's favorite flavor."

WPSU Coffee Break

"Our coffee ice cream with chocolate chips is named for Penn State's award-winning public broadcast TV channel."

Peanut Butter Cup

"Mmmmmm…. Chocolate and peanut butter, one of the best combinations of all time. We swirl peanut butter into our luxurious chocolate ice cream so you can get your flavor fix anytime."

Raspberry Parfait

"Red raspberry swirl oozes from our rich vanilla ice cream base for a delectable tartness you'll want to experience again and again."

Chocolate Frozen Yogurt

"Looking for that rich chocolate taste and mouthfeel but don’t want to splurge on fat? Indulge in the velvety richness of our lower-fat chocolate frozen yogurt."

Grilled Stickies

"We're complimenting a Penn State staple. Featuring a cinnamon bun flavor with sticky bun dough pieces and a cinnamon streusel swirl, this cool version of a favorite pastry will take you back to your college days (and nights)."

Pumpkin Pie

"Perfect for the fall holidays! A taste of our pumpkin ice cream with graham crunch pieces will leave you and your guests feeling blessed."

Black Raspberry Frozen Yogurt

"This lower-fat, tart frozen treat is just as delicious as the original fruit."

Coconut Chip

"The unmistakable sweet richness of coconut ice cream contrasts nicely with bittersweet chocolate chips, making this one of our most asked-for flavors."

Apple Cobbler Crunch

"Nothing tastes more homey than fresh apples baked with cinnamon and sugar and topped with delicious pastry. We've managed to recreate the all-American dish with cinnamon ice cream, graham crunch, and apple swirl."

Cookie Dough

"Put down the roll of raw dough - we've got something better. Our rich cookie dough ice cream with chunks of cookie dough and hearty chocolate chips is just as tasty - and a lot more dignified."

Bittersweet Mint

"More sweet than bitter, this Creamery favorite mixes peppermint ice cream with chips of indulgent dark chocolate for a cool, decadent mouthful of pure delight."

Cookies & Cream

"Vanilla ice cream, crunchy cookies. I know what I want."

Strawberry

"For when you know exactly what you want. Ripe strawberries and luxurious vanilla ice cream add up to simple perfection."

Chocolate

"Our quintessential chocolate ice cream is everything it should be - rich and creamy with just a touch of bitterness. Eat it alone or top with candy, fruit, or a hearty dollop of whipped cream for a little taste of heaven."

Vanilla

"If you're looking for a vanilla ice cream with noticeable dimension, look no further. This centennial celebration was created on the 100 year anniversary of the Creamery. A little extra richness from real vanilla bean makes it the perfect topping for birthday cake, pie, cobbler, or anything else your heart desires."
